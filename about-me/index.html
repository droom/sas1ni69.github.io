My name is Hassanin Ahmed, sas1ni69 on the interwebs and I'm a Ruby on Rails developer based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

I enjoy stand up comedy, singing, playing the guitar and Ultimate Frisbee. I strive to learn and to reinvent myself, over and over again. I've been developing for the web for the past 4 years and I've loved every day of it. I've had servers crash on me, databases wiped, code broke, clients threatening murder and about a thousand other software development crisis.

I am nothing without the people who taught me and gave me advice along the way, both offline and online, directly and indirectly. I try to write this blog as a reference to both myself and anyone who needs it. I also list my past and current projects on this site.

Say hi to me anytime, I would love to hear from fellow developers and tinkerers. I hope to give back as much as I've received and still receiving.
